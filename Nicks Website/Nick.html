  • About

I'm an experienced educator and lifelong learner with a passion for technology, languages, and self-development. I specialize in helping students build strong foundations and practical skills, especially in areas like language learning, computer science, and critical thinking. Outside of teaching, I enjoy working on personal tech projects—from building desktop apps to exploring cybersecurity and ethical hacking. I'm also deeply interested in philosophy, sci-fi storytelling, and staying active through activities like rock climbing and hunting. Whether I’m in the classroom or coding a new tool, I’m driven by curiosity, clarity, and a commitment to continuous growth.
